> ### All Features
1. Login with email or third party[Google,Apple]
2. Search Jobs ['titles']/['company'] with location
3. Job Recommendation based on your resume
4. Job [role full description,salary,location,company,skills,education]
5. Share job with people
6. Save job for later reference
7. Apply the job
8. Company review
9. Company can post jobs
10. My review section
11. Resume Service
12. Message Section
